Andrés Parra

Operations Director

Andres believes he is just a human being passionate about social change and entrepreneurship. Truly believing that everybody can contribute to make the world a better place. Currently based in Hanoi, Vietnam he’s co-starting the holistic entrepreneurship club from Hanoi to the world as a non-for-profit initiative to empower the youth from Vietnam to the world.

Along his career he has focused on the fields of social innovation building capacity cross sectors with a focus on Entrepreneurship Development, Leadership Enabling, Sustainable Development, Exponential Technologies, Design Thinking and Liberating Structures. In a nutshell: How to move forward the United Nation’s Sustainable Development Goals indicators through Social Business, that's all our work about.

Also, along with the journey, starting and engaged different projects, and participating in different programs around the world, in Mexico, Chile, Singapore, Hong Kong, Japan, Thailand, Afghanistan, Bangladesh, India and now Vietnam.

Andres has held leadership positions in the private, academic, a non-for-profit sectors; and he’s also a affiliated to 10+ international networks and communities in the content of sustainability, development and social empowerment.

Volunteering has been central to Andres’ life. Starting from very young in the Scouts of Mexico, and then volunteering in a diverse set of organizations like UN Volunteers and Dnet in Bangladesh, Intercultural Education in Hong Kong, Balloon Latam in Chile, HabilMind in Mexico, among others.

Andrés is always open to explore opportunities to collaborate and he’s currently co-starting Transcend Holistic Entrepreneurship Club in Hanoi while developing his own Social Innovation Consulting Firm, the Social Business Hub., SBH.

SPECIALITIES Social Enterprise Development Social Innovation Management Training, Capacity Building, and facilitation
