Thy-Diep ("Yip") Ta

Strategic Advisor

Yip is a first-generation German Vietnamese who is an author, entrepreneur coach and consultant. In her role as Germany’s first Chief Well-Being Officer, Yip creates the environment for Brainboost stakeholders (employees, customers, partners, fellow citizens) to do good and do well at the same time.

She is a certified mindfulness trainer, executive coach, venture capital (VC) and founders’ advisor and author of a leadership book and mindfulness manifesto from entrepreneurs for entrepreneurs. Yip has worked for/in government agencies, not-for-profit organizations, investment firms (VC), management consultancies, startups, and large corporations/family businesses.

She a network knitter, a matchmaker and a truly empathetic person who has joined this cause to drive change in Vietnamese youth by fundraising for the club and engaging in other leadership and management related activities.
